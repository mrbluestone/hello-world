Introduction to PowerPaint

Welcome to PowerPaint, your average paint program!

The best browser to use for this web app is Microsoft Edge, but all browsers are compatible.

Basic Controls

  1. To start painting, simply start pressing the left mouse button, then move it along the canvas. You have a drawing space that starts with the end of the word 'weight'until the end of the page.
  2. The first things you are going to alter are the color and the size of the brush. The first slider on the canvas notifies the amount of red you are putting into the color,going from 0 to 255.The second slider on the canvas notifies the amount of green you are putting into the color,going from 0 to 255.The third slider on the canvas notifies the amount of blue you are putting into the color,going from 0 to 255. The fourth slider is setting the size of the brush, with a radius going from 1 to 150.
